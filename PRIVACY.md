# Privacy Policy for CopyCache

**Last updated:** January 14, 2026

## Overview

CopyCache is a browser extension that saves your clipboard history locally on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Clipboard content**: Text and URLs that you copy while the extension is active

### What We DON'T Collect
- We do **not** collect any personal information
- We do **not** transmit any data to external servers
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking
- We do **not** share any data with third parties

## Data Storage

All clipboard history is stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). Your data never leaves your browser.

## Data Retention

- Clipboard history is stored until you manually delete it
- Use the "Clear All" button in the extension to delete all history
- Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data

## Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `clipboardRead` | Read clipboard contents to save history |
| `storage` | Store clipboard history locally |
| `activeTab` | Access current tab for clipboard operations |

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- View all stored clipboard entries in the extension popup
- Delete individual entries or clear all history
- Uninstall the extension to remove all data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ARJUNVARMA2000/CopyCache).
